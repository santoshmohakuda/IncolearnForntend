Guesstimates Course Curriculum

Eligibility

Ideal for individuals with a basic understanding of problem-solving and analytical skills. No prior experience in guesstimates is required.

  • Understand the fundamentals of guesstimates.
  • Apply estimation techniques to real-world scenarios.
  • Develop problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Practice with hands-on exercises.

Pre-requisites

Basic knowledge of mathematics and critical thinking skills. Familiarity with analytical reasoning is helpful but not required.

Module 01: Introduction to Guesstimates

  • - Overview of guesstimates and their importance
  • - Understanding the role of assumptions in guesstimates
  • - Basic techniques for breaking down problems
  • - Common frameworks and approaches (e.g., MECE, top-down vs. bottom-up)
  • - Practicing mental math and estimation skills

Module 02: Guesstimate Problem Solving

  • - Structuring guesstimate problems effectively
  • - Identifying and analyzing key drivers
  • - Approaches to different types of guesstimates (e.g., market sizing, population estimates)
  • - Using analogies and historical data for better estimates
  • - Practicing with real-world guesstimate examples

Module 03: Advanced Guesstimate Techniques

  • - Handling complex guesstimates with multiple variables
  • - Incorporating probabilistic thinking and ranges
  • - Developing confidence intervals for your estimates
  • - Dealing with uncertainty and ambiguous data
  • - Techniques for improving accuracy and refining estimates

Module 04: Application and Practice

  • - Case studies and guesstimate exercises across various industries
  • - Collaborative guesstimate problem-solving
  • - Presenting and defending your guesstimates
  • - Common pitfalls in guesstimates and how to avoid them
  • - Preparing for guesstimate interviews and assessments

Guesstimates Exam and Certification

The exam consists of multiple-choice questions and practical case studies. It is designed to test both theoretical knowledge and practical application of guesstimation techniques.

Review the course materials, complete practice exercises, and participate in study groups to enhance your understanding of guesstimation methods.

The certification is valid for three years. After this period, you may need to recertify or take additional training to maintain your certification status.

The cost of the certification exam is $200. This fee covers the exam attempt and includes access to online resources and practice materials.
