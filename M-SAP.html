SAP Exam and Certification

The SAP Certification Exam typically consists of multiple-choice questions, scenario-based queries, and practical assessments. The duration is around 180 minutes, and a minimum passing score of 70% is required.

Preparation for the SAP Certification includes studying official SAP training materials, attending online or classroom courses, and practicing with mock exams to gain confidence and proficiency.

Obtaining an SAP Certification enhances your professional credibility, opens doors to new job opportunities, and can lead to higher salaries and career advancement in the field of SAP.

SAP Certifications need to be renewed every two years to stay current with the latest updates and innovations. Recertification can be done by taking specific renewal assessments.
