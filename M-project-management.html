Project Management Course Curriculum

Eligibility

This course is ideal for professionals looking to advance in project management, including project coordinators, team leaders, and anyone pursuing PMP certification.

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline.
  • Basic understanding of business processes.
  • Work experience in project environments is a plus.
  • Commitment to completing the course requirements.

Pre-requisites

No prior project management experience is required, but familiarity with basic business concepts will be beneficial.

Module : 01

  • - Introduction to project management principles and methodologies
  • - Understanding the project lifecycle and phases
  • - Defining project scope, objectives, and deliverables
  • - Project charter development and stakeholder management
  • - Roles and responsibilities of project managers and team members

Module : 02

  • - Developing detailed project plans and timelines
  • - Resource allocation and management strategies
  • - Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and project scheduling techniques (Gantt charts, Critical Path Method)
  • - Risk management: identifying, analyzing, and mitigating risks
  • - Budgeting and financial management in projects

Module : 03

  • - Leading and motivating project teams
  • - Effective communication and collaboration practices
  • - Monitoring project progress and performance metrics
  • - Quality management and assurance techniques
  • - Change management and scope control

Module : 04

  • - Conducting project closure and final deliverable handover
  • - Post-project evaluation and capturing lessons learned
  • - Advanced project management tools and software
  • - Project management in different methodologies (Agile, Scrum, Lean)
  • - Preparing for advanced project management certifications (PMP, PRINCE2)

Project Management Exam and Certification

The exam covers project management fundamentals including project planning, execution, monitoring, and closure. It evaluates your understanding of project management processes and methodologies.

To obtain certification, you need to pass the exam and meet eligibility requirements which include specific educational qualifications and project management experience.

Study the PMBOK Guide, attend preparatory courses, and use practice exams to familiarize yourself with the exam format and key concepts.

Certification boosts your credibility, enhances career opportunities, and demonstrates your expertise in project management.
