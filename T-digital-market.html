Digital Marketing Course Curriculum

Eligibility

This course is ideal for professionals seeking to advance their digital marketing skills. Basic understanding of marketing concepts and familiarity with online platforms are recommended.

  • Understanding of marketing fundamentals.
  • Basic knowledge of web technologies.
  • Familiarity with social media platforms.
  • Willingness to learn and apply digital strategies.

Pre-requisites

Basic knowledge of marketing principles and some experience with digital tools is recommended but not required. A passion for digital marketing and a willingness to learn are essential.

FAQs

Digital Marketing encompasses online strategies like SEO, social media, and email marketing to reach and engage customers. It's essential for businesses to build their online presence and connect with their target audience.

Key skills include SEO, content creation, social media management, data analysis, and PPC advertising. Familiarity with tools like Google Analytics and social media platforms is also crucial.

Success is measured through metrics such as website traffic, conversion rates, click-through rates, and ROI. Using tools like Google Analytics can help track these key performance indicators.

SEO focuses on organic search results by optimizing your website for better visibility, while PPC involves paid advertising on search engines and other platforms to drive traffic to your site quickly.

Regular updates are crucial to keep up with market trends and algorithm changes. It is recommended to review and adjust your strategy at least quarterly or whenever major updates occur in your industry.
