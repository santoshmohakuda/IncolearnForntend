Digital Marketing Course Curriculum

Eligibility

To enroll in this Digital Marketing course, you should have a basic understanding of marketing principles and internet usage. No prior digital marketing experience is required, but a passion for learning and an eagerness to apply new strategies will be beneficial.

  • Familiarity with digital tools and platforms.
  • Basic knowledge of marketing concepts.
  • Access to a computer and internet connection.
  • Willingness to engage with course materials and assignments.

Pre-requisites

There are no specific pre-requisites for this course. However, a basic understanding of marketing and business concepts, as well as the ability to navigate online platforms, will enhance your learning experience.

Module : 01

  • - Introduction to Digital Marketing
  • - Overview of digital marketing
  • - Evolution and significance of digital marketing in the modern landscape
  • - Understanding the digital marketing ecosystem and key channels
  • - Creating a Digital Marketing Strategy
  • - Setting marketing goals and objectives

Module : 02

  • - Conducting a digital marketing audit
  • - Defining target audiences and buyer personas
  • - Website and User Experience Optimization
  • - Web design best practices for user experience
  • - Mobile responsiveness and optimization
  • - Conversion rate optimization (CRO) techniques
  • - Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • - Understanding search engine ranking factors

Module : 03

  • - On-page and off-page SEO optimization
  • - Keyword research and content optimization
  • - Introduction to PPC advertising platforms (e.g., Google Ads, Bing Ads)
  • - Creating effective ad campaigns and ad groups
  • - Utilizing major social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • - Content planning and creation for social media

Module : 4

  • - Understanding influencer marketing and its impact
  • - Identifying and collaborating with influencers
  • - Measuring influencer marketing ROI
  • - Managing online reputation and brand image
  • - Crisis communication strategies

Digital Marketing Exam and Certification

The digital marketing exam consists of multiple-choice questions, case studies, and practical scenarios. It tests your knowledge of various digital marketing strategies, tools, and best practices.

Certification is awarded upon successful completion of the exam and meeting the minimum passing score. You will receive a digital certificate that you can share on your professional profiles.

There are no formal prerequisites for the exam. However, having a foundational knowledge of digital marketing concepts and tools is recommended for better preparation and performance.

We provide a range of resources including study guides, practice exams, and online tutorials. Additionally, there are recommended books and online courses to help you prepare thoroughly for the exam.
